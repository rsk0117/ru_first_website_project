Distance Education Admission Requirement Criteria (Minimum Level of Prior Education, Preparation, Training)

Evaluation of an applicant’s ability to pursue study at The Reformed University are based on prior college/university credits, examinations, advanced placement scores, prior learning and other factors that may be indicative of academic success. The school will evaluate in accordance with established university guidance, and each application will be individually evaluated. The Reformed University considers all applications for admission. The Reformed University reserves the right to reject any applicant who in the judgment of University Admission Personnel, appears unprepared to do academic study at The Reformed University based on evaluation of the individual’s application and available evidence of aptitude.

Applicants must present the following materials to the Registrar.
The Registrar is located at 3010 Wilshire Blvd. Los Angeles, California 90010.

All students who apply for our Distance Education program must submit the followings: ? A completed and signed Application
? $100 Non-refundable Application Fee
? $100 Non-refundable Registration Fee

Also, prospective students should: ? Have online access,
? Have basic computer skills (Know how to send/receive e-mail, send/receive e-mail attachments, read and follow
  directions on a website/a computer, create documents on Microsoft Word, and etc),
? Have ability to send and receive lesson materials through US Postal Service (USPS).

BACHELOR OF ARTS IN THEOLOGICAL STUDIES All applicants for Bachelor of Arts in Theological Studies must show evidence of successful completion of a high school education or its equivalent, such as completion of a General Education Development (GED) Test. Presentation of an official high school transcript or GED Diploma will fulfill this requirement.

Additional Requirements: 1) Sealed and unopened official high school transcript or GED Diploma
2) A letter of recommendation

 

 

? MASTER OF ARTS IN THEOLOGICAL STUDIES All applicants for the Master of Arts in Theological Studies must show evidence of successful completion of a Bachelor’s Degree. Additional Requirements: 1) Sealed and unopend official transcript
2) Evidence of completion of a Bachelor Degree (i.e. Diploma)
3) A letter of recommendation

 

 

MASTER OF DIVINITY All applicants for the Master of Divinity mush show evidence of successful completion of a Bachelor’s Degree. Additional Requirements: 1) Sealed and unopened official transcript
2) Evidence of completion of a Bachelor Degree (i.e. Diploma)
3) A pastoral recommendation
4) Personal statement (Testimony)

 

 

? DOCTOR OF MINISTRY All applicants for the Doctor of Ministry must show evidence of successful completion of a Master’s Degree. Additional requirements: 1) Sealed and unopened official transcript
2) Evidence of completion of a Master of Divinity or equivalent(i.e. Diploma)
3) Two pastoral recomentdations
4) Personal statement (Testimony)
5) Valid proof of demonstration of three years of experience in a full time ministry after receiving a Master of Divinity Degree

 

 

? DOCTOR OF THEOLOGY All applicants for the Doctor of Theology must show evidence of successful completion of a Master’s Degree. Additional requirements: 1) Sealed and unopened official transcript
2) Evidence of completion of a Master's Degree (i.e. Diploma)
3) Two pastoral / Academic recommentdations
4) Personal statement (Testimony)
5) Professional vitae
6) Interview with the Doctoral Committee
7) GRE: 400 Verbal and 560 Quantitative (if taken prior to August 1, 2011),
         146 Verbal and 146 Quantitative (if taken on or after August 1, 2011),
         3.5 Analytical Writing
   or TOEFL: 550 PBT, 80 iBT (minimum 20 on each section)
8) Overall G.P.A. 2.5 on a 4.0 scale
9) Writing sample: 5-10 pages long major writing sample/research paper, completed within 5 years from the application deadline.
