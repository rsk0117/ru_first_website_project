Admission Procedure

General Admissions Policy

Potential applicants should contact The Reformed University for any admission inquiry and meet with an admissions representative. The representative will provide detailed information on the institution's programs and policies, discuss the applicant's qualifications, determining the best way to meet his/her personal, intellectual and spiritual objectives. The best way to find out if the school is right for you is by visiting the campus, however if the inquiry is made by phone or email, the admissions coordinator can guide through a school welcome package which includes the admissions procedure and detailed information about the University.

Applicants must submit the following materials to the Registrar. Please send your application to 3010 Wilshire Blvd. Los Angeles, California 90010.

Standards Application Requirements

Applicants are evaluated on the basis of academic abilities, professional goals, character, and commitment. Submission of completed and signed application for admission including,
  • Religious Autobiography
  • Official transcript from post secondary school attended
  • Recommendation letter
  • Applicants of Master's programs must hold a Bachelor's Degree or its equivalent
  • Applicants of Doctorate programs must hold a Master's Degree or its equivalent

Undergraduate Program Applicants

  • A completed and signed Application form
  • Recommendation Letters (2)
  • Religious Autobiography (Personal and Religious Background with Vision Statement)
  • Official transcript and copy of diploma of High School or its equivalent
  • $100 Non-refundable Application Fee
  • $100 Non-refundable Registration Fee
*All applicants for Undergraduate Program must show evidence of successful completion of a high school education or its equivalent, such as completion of a General Education Development (GED) Test. Presentation of an official high school transcript or GED Diploma will fulfill this requirement.


Graduate Program Applicants

  • A completed and signed Application Form
  • Recommendation Letters (3)
  • Religious Autobiography (Personal and Religious Background with Vision Statement)
  • Post secondary college-level official transcript which indicates Bachelor's / Master's degree is granted
  • Master's: Copy of Bachelor's Diploma / Doctor's: Copy of Master's Diploma
  • $100 Non-refundable Application Fee
  • $100 Non-refundable Registration Fee
